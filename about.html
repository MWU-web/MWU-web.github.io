About Us

Established in Nairobi Kenya since 2013 to represent transport workers in the Matatu Industry both formal and informal, Men and Women

Home About

MISSION

Matatu Workers Union. Dedicated to championing the rights of Matau workers, promoting inclusivity and driving change in the Matatu industry

VISION

To be a leader in championing of rights for the matatu workers .

WHY WE NEED TRADE UNIONS IN THE MATATU INDUSTRY.

Most of us in the matatu indusrty face a variety of problems which include low pay , long working hours, insecurity, Harrassment from police and authorities, Exloitation by owners, Lack of social protection. By coming together, we are a strong voice for change. This enables us to adequately air our views.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Enforce compliance with the law

To ensure that our drivers and conductors have complied with traffic rules and regulations

POLICY MAKING

To contrtribute to the development, adoption and effective implementation of national policies, laws and programs which are beneficial to workers

FINANCE

To participate in national development through promotion among members of cooperatives, saving and credit societies, welfare groups, establishment of invest and benovelent funds

INCENTIVIZATION

To provide incentives and enjhance a sense of responsibility in the members to enhance a sense of responsibility among members

COLLECTIVE BARGAIN

To establish a collective barganing machinery

EDUCATION

To improve knowledge, skills and efficiency of members by way of education and training through the union

JOB SECURITY

To strive for full employment in quality jobs and to protect members from unfair dismisals, retrenchment, unwanted transfers and loss of jobs in aother ways

UNIONISM

To express solidarity with other unions globally and engage in lawful activities, promote mutual respect and support among unions and workers in general
