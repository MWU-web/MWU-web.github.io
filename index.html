WHY WE NEED TRADE UNIONS IN THE MATATU INDUSTRY.

Most of us in the matatu indusrty face a variety of problems which include low pay , long working hours, insecurity, Harrassment from police and authorities, Exloitation by owners, Lack of social protection. By coming together, we are a strong voice for change. This enables us to adequately air our views.

Benefits of joining a trade union

Respect of rights and improvement of our pay and working conditions

Respect of rights and improvement of our pay and working conditions is a core mandate for the trade Union. That members have an active voice in defending their salaries and demand increases for improvement of living standards

Experience a community environment.

Connect with other members in our proffesion. Share Ideas and suggestions with people in your field.
